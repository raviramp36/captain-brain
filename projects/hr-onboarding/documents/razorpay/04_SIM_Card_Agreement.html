PREAMBLE

This Agreement is entered into between [Company_Name] ("Company") and [Employee_Name] ("Employee") regarding the use of a Company SIM card registered under the name of a Company Director or authorized representative.

The Employee acknowledges that the SIM card is registered under [SIM_Registered_Name] for regulatory compliance purposes and is being provided to the Employee solely for business use.

1. PURPOSE AND PERMITTED USE

1.1 The SIM card is provided to the Employee exclusively for:

  • Official business communications (calls, SMS, data)
  • Client and vendor interactions
  • Team coordination and internal communications
  • Access to Company applications and systems requiring mobile verification

1.2 The Employee agrees to use the SIM card solely for Company business purposes.

2. PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES

2.1 The Employee shall NOT use the SIM card for:

  • Personal financial transactions including banking, UPI, mobile wallets, loan applications, or any financial services
  • Personal subscriptions including OTT platforms, gaming, dating apps, or any paid services
  • Social media registrations using this number as the primary or recovery number for personal accounts
  • Any illegal activities including harassment, threats, fraud, or any activity that violates laws
  • Political activities or communications that could implicate the Company or its Directors
  • Commercial activities unrelated to Company business
  • Sharing or lending the SIM card to any third party
  • Porting the number or attempting to change registration details

3. SECURITY OBLIGATIONS

3.1 The Employee shall:

  • Keep the SIM card secure at all times
  • Report loss or theft immediately to the Company (within 2 hours)
  • Not share OTPs, verification codes, or any authentication received on this number
  • Enable SIM PIN protection where available
  • Not use the number for two-factor authentication of personal accounts

4. MONITORING AND AUDIT

4.1 The Employee acknowledges that:

  • The Company may monitor usage of the SIM card
  • Call records and data usage may be reviewed for compliance
  • Any misuse may be investigated and documented
  • The Company retains the right to audit usage at any time

5. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION

5.1 The Employee acknowledges that the SIM card is registered under a Company Director's name and that any misuse may directly implicate the Director and the Company.

5.2 The Employee hereby agrees to INDEMNIFY and HOLD HARMLESS the Company, its Directors, officers, and representatives from and against:

  • Any claims, damages, or liabilities arising from the Employee's use or misuse of the SIM card
  • Legal costs, penalties, or fines resulting from violations by the Employee
  • Regulatory actions or investigations triggered by the Employee's activities
  • Any third-party claims related to the Employee's use of the SIM card
  • Reputational damage to the Company or its Directors

5.3 This indemnification shall include all reasonable legal fees, costs, and expenses incurred in defending against any claims.

5.4 This indemnification obligation survives termination of employment.

6. RETURN OF SIM CARD

6.1 The Employee shall return the SIM card to the Company:

  • Upon termination or resignation (on the last working day)
  • Upon request by the Company at any time
  • Upon transfer to a role that does not require the SIM card

6.2 Failure to return the SIM card may result in:

  • Deduction from final settlement
  • Reporting to telecom authorities
  • Legal action for recovery

7. CONSEQUENCES OF MISUSE

7.1 Misuse of the SIM card may result in:

  • Immediate confiscation of the SIM card
  • Disciplinary action including termination
  • Recovery of costs and damages from the Employee
  • Legal action where warranted
  • Reporting to law enforcement if illegal activities are suspected
