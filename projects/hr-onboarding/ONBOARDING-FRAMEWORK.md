## PART B: PROTECTION DOCUMENTS STRUCTURE

### DOCUMENT 1: NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (NDA)

**Purpose:** Protect all confidential business information

**Structure:**
```
1. DEFINITIONS
   - "Confidential Information" (comprehensive definition)
   - "Disclosing Party" / "Receiving Party"
   - "Purpose"

2. SCOPE OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION
   2.1 Client Information
       - Client names, contacts, requirements
       - Project details, deliverables, pricing
       - Client communications
   2.2 Financial Information
       - Revenue, costs, margins, projections
       - Pricing strategies, quotations
       - Vendor/supplier terms
   2.3 Salary & HR Information
       - Own salary details
       - Colleagues' salary information
       - HR policies, increments, bonuses
   2.4 Business Information
       - Strategies, plans, roadmaps
       - Proposals, pitches, presentations
       - Internal processes, SOPs
   2.5 Technical Information
       - Proprietary code, designs, algorithms
       - Product specifications
       - R&D activities
   2.6 Third-Party Information
       - Information received from clients/vendors under NDA

3. OBLIGATIONS
   3.1 Non-Disclosure
   3.2 Non-Use (except for job duties)
   3.3 Protection measures
   3.4 Need-to-know basis only
   3.5 No copies without authorization
   3.6 Mandatory breach reporting

4. EXCLUSIONS
   - Publicly available information
   - Information already known
   - Information received from third party legally
   - Information required by law (with notice)

5. TERM
   - During employment: Absolute
   - Post-employment: 3 years

6. RETURN OF MATERIALS
   - Upon termination, return/delete all confidential info

7. REMEDIES
   - Injunctive relief
   - Damages
   - Legal costs recovery

8. ACKNOWLEDGMENT
   - Employee understands and agrees
   - Signature, date, witness
```

### DOCUMENT 2: NON-COMPETE & NON-SOLICITATION AGREEMENT

**Purpose:** Prevent employees from joining competitors, poaching staff/clients

**Structure:**
```
1. DEFINITIONS
   - "Competitor" (named list + description)
   - "Client" 
   - "Employee"
   - "Confidential Information"

2. DURING EMPLOYMENT
   2.1 Non-Compete
       - Cannot work for competitors (including consulting)
       - Cannot start competing business
       - Cannot hold interest in competing business
   2.2 Non-Solicitation of Employees
       - Cannot recruit colleagues for other companies
       - Cannot encourage colleagues to leave
   2.3 Non-Solicitation of Clients
       - Cannot divert business
       - Cannot provide services to clients independently
   2.4 Moonlighting Prohibition
       - No secondary employment without written approval
       - Especially prohibited with competitors

3. POST-EMPLOYMENT (12-24 months)
   3.1 Non-Solicitation of Employees
       - Cannot recruit ex-colleagues
       - Cannot facilitate their recruitment
   3.2 Non-Solicitation of Clients
       - Cannot solicit clients known during employment
       - Cannot accept work from such clients
   3.3 Non-Compete Advisory
       - Strong advisory against joining competitors
       - (Note: Limited enforceability under Indian law, but acts as deterrent)

4. NAMED COMPETITORS LIST
   - Jaseil Creations
   - 4Brains
   - Tagbin
   - MagicTap
   - InkinnCaps
   - Attah
   - Digital Jalebi
   - [Others as identified]

5. CONSIDERATION
   - Employment itself is consideration
   - Access to confidential information & training

6. REASONABLENESS
   - Geographic scope: India + countries of operation
   - Time period: Reasonable (12-24 months post-exit)
   - Scope: Limited to actual competing business

7. REMEDIES

   - Damages including lost revenue

8. SEVERABILITY
   - If any clause unenforceable, others remain valid
```

### DOCUMENT 3: SALARY CONFIDENTIALITY DECLARATION

**Purpose:** Prevent salary discussions and comparisons internally

**Structure:**
```
1. DECLARATION
   - Acknowledges salary is strictly confidential
   - Determined by company based on role, experience, performance

2. OBLIGATIONS
   2.1 Non-Disclosure of Own Salary
       - Cannot share with colleagues
       - Cannot share with external parties
       - Cannot post on social media/Glassdoor etc.
   2.2 Non-Inquiry of Others' Salary
       - Cannot seek colleagues' salary information
       - Cannot pressure others to disclose
   2.3 Non-Discussion
       - Cannot discuss salary comparisons
       - Cannot create salary-related discord

3. EXCEPTIONS
   - Discussion with HR for clarifications
   - Legal requirements
   - Spouse/immediate family (with confidentiality advisory)

4. CONSEQUENCES OF BREACH
   - First offense: Written warning
   - Second offense: Suspension
   - Third offense: Termination
   - Legal action if damages occur

5. ACKNOWLEDGMENT
   - Understands and agrees
   - Signature, date
```

### DOCUMENT 4: COMPANY SIM CARD USAGE AGREEMENT

**Purpose:** Protect directors whose names are on company SIM registrations

**Structure:**
```
1. DETAILS
   - Employee name
   - Mobile number assigned
   - SIM serial/ICCID number
   - Registered owner (Director's name)
   - Date of assignment

2. ACKNOWLEDGMENT
   - SIM registered under Director's name for company purposes
   - Employee is custodian, not owner
   - Company property at all times

3. PERMITTED USE
   - Official business communication only
   - Client interactions
   - Team coordination
   - As directed by company

4. PROHIBITED USE
   - Personal use beyond incidental
   - Registration for personal accounts (UPI, social media, etc.)
   - Any illegal activities
   - Harassment, fraud, defamation
   - Bulk messaging/spam
   - Any activity that could harm company/director reputation
   - Sharing SIM/number with third parties

5. MONITORING
   - Company may monitor usage
   - Bill review and audit rights
   - No expectation of privacy for business communications

6. RESPONSIBILITY & LIABILITY
   - Employee fully responsible for all activities on the number
   - Employee liable for any misuse
   - Employee to immediately report loss/theft
   - Employee to report any suspicious activity

7. INDEMNITY
   - Employee indemnifies Company and Director against:
     - Any legal action arising from misuse
     - Any financial loss
     - Any reputational damage
     - Regulatory penalties
   - Indemnity survives termination

8. RETURN & HANDOVER
   - Must return SIM on last working day
   - Must provide all linked accounts list
   - Must transfer/delink all business accounts
   - Failure to return: Recovery of SIM cost + damages

9. POST-EMPLOYMENT
   - Number remains company property
   - Cannot claim ownership/portability
   - Cannot continue using post-exit

10. DIRECTOR PROTECTION CLAUSE
    - Acknowledges Director has no control over employee's actions
    - Employee solely liable for any violations
    - Employee waives any claim against Director personally

11. SIGNATURES
    - Employee
    - HR Witness
    - Date
```

### DOCUMENT 5: INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY

**Purpose:** Rules for handling company data and systems

**Structure:**
```
1. SCOPE
   - Applies to all employees, contractors, interns
   - Covers all company data, systems, devices

2. DATA CLASSIFICATION
   2.1 Highly Confidential
       - Financial data, salary info, client contracts
       - Strategy documents, pricing
   2.2 Confidential  
       - Client communications, project details
       - Internal processes, SOPs
   2.3 Internal
       - General communications
       - Non-sensitive operational data
   2.4 Public
       - Marketing materials, website content

3. RULES
   3.1 Device Security
       - Strong passwords required
       - Screen lock mandatory
       - No company data on personal devices without approval
       - Immediate report of loss/theft
   
   3.2 Data Handling
       - No forwarding to personal email
       - No unauthorized copies
       - No screenshots of sensitive data
       - Secure disposal of printed materials
   
   3.3 Communication
       - Official channels only for business data
       - No sharing via personal WhatsApp/email
       - No discussion in public places
   
   3.4 Access Control
       - Need-to-know basis
       - No sharing of login credentials
       - Immediate password change if compromised
   
   3.5 External Interactions
       - No disclosure to ex-employees
       - Report any approach by competitors
       - Clear desk policy
   
   3.6 Social Media
       - No company information without approval
       - No photos of work/office with sensitive info visible
       - No negative posts about company/clients

4. REPORTING
   - Report security incidents immediately to HR/Management
   - Whistleblower protection for good-faith reports

5. MONITORING
   - Company may monitor company systems, email, devices
   - Consent to monitoring acknowledged

6. CONSEQUENCES
   - Policy violations = disciplinary action
   - Severity-based: Warning → Suspension → Termination → Legal

7. ACKNOWLEDGMENT
   - Signature, date
```
