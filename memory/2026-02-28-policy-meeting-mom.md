## 📋 Key Decisions

### 1. Replacement Leave Policy
- Event executors earn replacement leave for Sundays/holidays worked
- Non-executors need team lead approval to qualify
- Tracked in payroll system year-round

### 2. Late Check-In Policy
- Office hours: **10:30 AM start** with 30-minute grace period
- Arrivals after grace period → warnings → escalating to salary impact
- Meeting start must be sharp at 10:30

### 3. Leave Approval Structure
- Team leads are **final approvers** for all leave requests
- Employees must report to leads first
- HR & management only for escalations
- No self-granted leaves allowed

### 4. Leave Categories Defined

| Type | Rules |
|------|-------|
| Casual | For personal needs, requires advance notice |
| Sick | For health issues, documentation may be required |
| Earned | Accrued over time, requires planning |

### 5. Reimbursement Process
- **Biweekly processing** to eliminate delays
- Imprest amounts capped per person to prevent large outstanding expenses
- Late bill uploads causing delays — must be fixed

### 6. Company Calendar
- Formal calendar to be established post-financial year-end
- Standardizes holidays & office events
- Improves budget planning & employee awareness
