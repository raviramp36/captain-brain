# Craftech360 HR Policies & Processes

*Source: Suman N (HR Lead) — 2026-02-07*

---

## Attendance Tracking

- **Tool:** Razorpay (HR/Payroll module)
- **Process:** Employees mark attendance regularly, HR monitors logs
- **Issues:** Occasional delays in marking, last-minute leave intimation
- **Resolution:** Follow-ups, manager involvement, policy reinforcement

---

## Leave Policy

| Leave Type | Balance / Total |
|------------|-----------------|
| Work From Home | 6 / 6 |
| Casual Leave | 15 / 15 |
| Sick Leave | 9 / 9 |
| Special Leave | 2 / 2 |
| Replacement Off | 5 / 5 |
| Paternity Leave | 7 / 7 |
| Half Day Leave | 2 / 2 |

**Total Annual Leave:** ~46 days (excluding WFH)

## Onboarding Process

**Duration:** 1-2 working days (core), first week for role-specific alignment

**Checklist:**
1. Offer letter & acceptance
2. Document collection (ID, address proof, education, bank details)
3. HR policy & office guidelines briefing
4. Role introduction & reporting structure
5. Tool/system access coordination
6. Team introduction

---

## Performance Management

**Review Cycles:**
- Quarterly formal reviews
- Monthly informal check-ins by reporting managers

**KPI Tracking:**
- Role-based deliverables
- Project timelines & output quality
- Manager feedback
- Attendance and work discipline

**Current Method:** Manual, review-based, supported by team leads

---

## Pain Points (Opportunities for Improvement)

1. **Manual tracking** — Attendance, leave, follow-ups across departments
2. **Hiring follow-ups** — Time-consuming coordination
3. **Decentralized systems** — Need centralized HR system

**What would help:**
- Centralized HR system for attendance, leave, performance
- Clearer ownership from team leads for routine follow-ups
- Automation in hiring trackers and onboarding documentation

---

## Recruitment Process

**Sourcing Channels:**
- Job portals (Naukri, etc.)
- LinkedIn
- Employee referrals
- Institutes/campus

**Interview Flow:**
1. Manpower requirement approval (dept head + management)
2. HR sources & shortlists candidates
3. Initial HR screening
4. Technical/functional interview
5. Final selection (management approval)
6. Offer letter
7. Onboarding formalities

---

## Exit / Offboarding Process

**Steps:**
1. Formal resignation from employee
2. Notice period tracking
3. Handover of responsibilities
4. Asset return
5. System access revocation
6. Exit interview (feedback collection)
7. Departmental clearance
8. Full & final settlement
9. Relieving documents issued

---

## Training & Development

**Types:**
- Onboarding training (culture, policies, tools)
- Role-specific skill enhancement
- Continuous learning

**Methods:**
- Internal sessions
- Hands-on project exposure
- Performance-based learning plans

**Goal:** Support career growth

- Structured reporting hierarchy
- Defined roles and reporting lines per department
- HR coordinates with department heads and leadership
- Focus areas: Workforce planning, performance management, employee engagement
