🚨 Current Pain Points

⚡ BD — Follow-up Issues

  • No regular updates on what they're doing
  • Payment follow-up commitments not tracked
  • Things promised but not delivered
  • Projects lost due to dropped follow-ups

✈️ Operations — Travel & Logistics

  • No tracking of hotel bookings → miss early discounts
  • No tracking of flights → paying higher costs
  • Unorganized planning
  • DC management needs improvement

  • No proper communication channel

  • Information scattered across chats
