## Status Indicators

| Status | Meaning | When to Use |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 🟢 On Track | Everything proceeding well | All logistics done, dev on track |
| 🟡 In Progress | Some gaps to address | Missing travel/DC/inventory |
| 🔴 At Risk | Critical issues | Major blockers, deadline at risk |
