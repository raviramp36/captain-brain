1. INTRODUCTION

This Code of Conduct establishes the behavioral and ethical standards expected of all employees of [Company_Name]. It reflects our commitment to creating a professional, respectful, and legally compliant workplace.

Our Core Values: Excellence | Integrity | Innovation | Respect | Collaboration

2. PROFESSIONAL CONDUCT

2.1 Workplace Behavior

  • Treat colleagues, clients, and partners with respect and dignity
  • Maintain professional demeanor in all interactions
  • Communicate clearly, honestly, and constructively
  • Take responsibility for actions and decisions
  • Support team members and contribute positively to team dynamics
  • Dress appropriately for work environment and client interactions

2.2 Attendance and Punctuality

  • Report to work on time as per assigned schedule
  • Notify supervisor promptly in case of absence or delay
  • Respect meeting times and deadlines
  • Complete assigned working hours faithfully
  • Request leave through proper channels in advance

2.3 Quality and Performance

  • Deliver work that meets or exceeds quality standards
  • Meet deadlines and communicate proactively about challenges
  • Continuously improve skills and knowledge
  • Take ownership of projects and see them through to completion

3. PROHIBITED CONDUCT

ZERO TOLERANCE: The following behaviors will result in immediate disciplinary action, up to and including termination without notice.

3.1 Harassment and Discrimination

  • Sexual harassment - unwelcome sexual advances, requests for sexual favors, or verbal/physical conduct of a sexual nature
  • Workplace bullying - repeated intimidation, humiliation, or offensive behavior
  • Discrimination - based on gender, religion, caste, race, ethnicity, age, disability, or sexual orientation
  • Retaliation - against anyone who reports violations or participates in investigations

3.2 Substance Abuse

  • Possession, use, or distribution of illegal drugs on Company premises
  • Reporting to work under the influence of alcohol or drugs
  • Consumption of alcohol during work hours without authorization

3.3 Violence and Threats

  • Physical violence or threats of violence
  • Possession of weapons on Company premises
  • Intimidating or threatening behavior
  • Damage to Company or personal property

3.4 Dishonesty and Fraud

  • Theft or misappropriation of Company assets
  • Falsification of records, time sheets, or expense reports
  • Providing false information during hiring or employment
  • Bribery or acceptance of improper payments
  • Forgery of signatures or documents

4. CONFLICTS OF INTEREST

Employees must avoid situations where personal interests conflict with Company interests:

  • Disclose any outside employment, business interests, or board positions
  • Do not engage in business relationships with competitors without approval
  • Avoid financial relationships with vendors or clients that could influence decisions
  • Do not use Company resources for personal gain
  • Disclose family relationships with clients, vendors, or competitors
  • Seek approval before accepting gifts valued over INR 2,500

5. SOCIAL MEDIA AND EXTERNAL COMMUNICATION

  • Do not speak on behalf of the Company without authorization
  • Clearly identify personal opinions as your own on social media
  • Do not share confidential Company information publicly
  • Do not post content that could damage the Company's reputation
  • Direct media inquiries to authorized spokespersons

6. CLIENT RELATIONS

  • Represent the Company professionally at all times
  • Deliver on commitments and communicate proactively
  • Maintain confidentiality of client information
  • Escalate issues appropriately rather than making unauthorized commitments
  • Treat client property and premises with respect
  • Adhere to client site rules and security protocols

8. REPORTING VIOLATIONS

Report violations or concerns through:

  • Direct Supervisor - for general workplace issues
  • HR Department - [HR_Email]
  • Management - for serious concerns
  • Internal Complaints Committee (ICC) - for POSH-related complaints
