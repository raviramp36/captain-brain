1. PURPOSE

This Policy establishes rules for software usage, licensing compliance, and proper use of Company email to protect [Company_Name] from legal, financial, and reputational risks arising from unauthorized software use or email misuse.

2. SOFTWARE COMPLIANCE

2.1 General Rules:

  • Only use software that is properly licensed by the Company
  • Do not install any software without IT approval
  • Do not download software from unofficial or untrusted sources
  • Respect all software license terms and conditions
  • Report any license violations you become aware of

2.2 STRICTLY PROHIBITED:

  • Installing, using, or distributing pirated or unlicensed software
  • Using cracked, keygen, or patch software to bypass licensing
  • Downloading software from torrent sites or illegal sources
  • Sharing Company software licenses with unauthorized users
  • Installing personal software on Company devices without approval
  • Circumventing software activation or validation mechanisms

3. COMPANY EMAIL USAGE

3.1 Permitted Use:

Company email ([Employee_Email]) is provided for:

  • Official business communications
  • Client and vendor correspondence
  • Internal team collaboration
  • Registration for Company-approved services and tools

3.2 PROHIBITED USE:

Company email shall NOT be used for:

  • Registering for pirated software, crack sites, or illegal services
  • Personal subscriptions (OTT, gaming, social media, dating apps)
  • Personal financial services (banking, trading, crypto)
  • Any service that violates laws or regulations
  • Services that could expose the Company to legal liability
  • Mass mailing, spam, or chain letters
  • Any activity that misrepresents the Company

4. PERSONAL LIABILITY CLAUSE

IMPORTANT - READ CAREFULLY:

4.1 The Employee acknowledges that using pirated software or registering Company email for unauthorized services can result in:

  • Legal notices and demands from software vendors
  • Lawsuits against the Company for copyright infringement
  • Heavy penalties and fines
  • Reputational damage to the Company

4.2 PERSONAL LIABILITY: If the Employee:

  • Uses pirated or unlicensed software on Company systems, OR
  • Uses Company email to register for pirated software or unauthorized services, OR
  • Engages in any activity that results in legal claims against the Company related to software piracy or email misuse

Then the Employee shall be PERSONALLY LIABLE for:

  • All penalties, fines, and damages claimed against the Company
  • Legal costs and attorney fees incurred by the Company
  • Settlement amounts paid by the Company
  • Any other losses suffered by the Company as a result

4.3 INDEMNIFICATION: The Employee hereby agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Company, its Directors, officers, and employees from all claims, damages, losses, costs, and expenses (including legal fees) arising from the Employee's violation of this Policy.

4.4 This indemnification obligation survives termination of employment.

5. AUDIT AND MONITORING

5.1 The Company reserves the right to:

  • Audit software installed on Company devices
  • Monitor email usage for compliance
  • Investigate suspected violations
  • Remove unauthorized software without notice

5.2 The Employee consents to such auditing and monitoring as a condition of employment.

6. REPORTING OBLIGATIONS

6.1 The Employee must immediately report to IT:

  • Any suspected software license violations
  • Receipt of legal notices related to software
  • Awareness of pirated software use by any employee
  • Any suspicious software or unauthorized installations

7. CONSEQUENCES OF VIOLATION

7.1 Violation of this Policy may result in:

  • Immediate termination of employment
  • Recovery of damages from the Employee
  • Legal action including criminal proceedings where applicable
  • Reporting to law enforcement for copyright violations
  • Negative reference for future employment

7.2 The Company will not shield employees from legal consequences of their illegal actions.
