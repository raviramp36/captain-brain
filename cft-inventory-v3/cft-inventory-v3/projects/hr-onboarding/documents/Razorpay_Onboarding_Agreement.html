PART B: NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT (NDA)

7. Confidential Information

The Employee acknowledges that during employment, they will have access to confidential and proprietary information of the Company. "Confidential Information" includes, but is not limited to:

  • Client lists, contact details, project specifications, and pricing
  • Financial data, business plans, and strategic information
  • Technical designs, source code, and proprietary methodologies
  • Employee information, salary details, and organizational data
  • Trade secrets, vendor relationships, and competitive analysis
  • Any information designated as confidential by the Company

8. Non-Disclosure Obligations

The Employee agrees to:

  1. Hold all Confidential Information in strict confidence
  2. Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without prior written authorization
  3. Use Confidential Information solely for performing job duties
  4. Not copy, reproduce, or remove Confidential Information except as required for work
  5. Return all Confidential Information upon termination of employment
  6. Immediately report any suspected breach of confidentiality

These obligations survive the termination of employment for a period of three (3) years.

PART C: SALARY CONFIDENTIALITY

9. Salary Non-Disclosure

The Employee agrees that their compensation, benefits, and related financial information are strictly confidential. The Employee shall NOT:

  • Discuss their salary with other employees or external parties
  • Share salary slips, offer letters, or compensation documents
  • Inquire about other employees' compensation
  • Post compensation details on social media or job portals

Consequences of Breach: Violation may result in disciplinary action including verbal warning, written warning, and termination for repeat offenses.

PART D: NON-COMPETE & NON-SOLICITATION

10. Non-Compete

During employment and for a period of [Non_Compete_Period] after termination, the Employee shall not directly or indirectly engage with any competing business that offers similar products or services, including but not limited to companies in the experiential technology, kinetic installations, and AI experiences industry.

11. Non-Solicitation

For a period of twelve (12) months following termination, the Employee shall not:

  • Solicit, recruit, or induce any Company employee to leave
  • Solicit or divert any client or customer with whom the Employee had dealings
  • Interfere with any business relationship of the Company

PART E: INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY

12. Data Security Obligations

The Employee agrees to:

  • Use strong passwords (minimum 12 characters) and enable multi-factor authentication
  • Lock workstation when unattended; screen lock after 5 minutes of inactivity
  • Store Company data only on approved systems (Company Google Drive, authorized cloud services)
  • Report lost/stolen devices to IT immediately (within 1 hour)
  • Not use personal email for Company business
  • Not install unauthorized software on Company devices

13. Prohibited Activities

The Employee shall NOT:

  • Share login credentials with anyone
  • Access Company systems from public WiFi without VPN
  • Transfer Company data to personal devices without authorization
  • Use Company systems for illegal activities

PART F: IT & SOFTWARE POLICY

14. Software Compliance

The Employee acknowledges and agrees:

  • Use of pirated, unlicensed, or unauthorized software is strictly prohibited
  • Company email shall be used for official Company business ONLY
  • The Employee shall NOT use Company email to register for personal services, pirated software, or unauthorized platforms
  • Only Company-licensed software shall be installed on Company devices

15. Personal Liability for Violations

IMPORTANT: If the Employee violates software licensing terms or uses Company email for unauthorized purposes, resulting in legal claims, penalties, or damages against the Company, the Employee shall be personally liable for all such claims and shall indemnify the Company against any losses incurred.
