## 📅 Daily & Weekly Rhythms

### Daily Stand-up (10:00 AM IST)
- **Who:** All department leads (5 min each)
- **Format:** What's done, what's planned, any blockers
- **Owner:** Pattar chairs (or Shivu if Pattar unavailable)

### Weekly Sync (Monday 11:00 AM)
- **Who:** Pattar, Abilash, Pradeep, Department Leads
- **Agenda:** 
  - Project status review
  - Pipeline & revenue update
  - Blockers & decisions needed
  - Week's priorities

### Weekly Report to Ravi (Friday 6:00 PM)
- **Owner:** Pattar
- **Format:** 5-bullet summary via WhatsApp
  - Revenue this week
  - Projects delivered/in-progress
  - Any issues handled
  - Key decisions made
  - Next week's focus
