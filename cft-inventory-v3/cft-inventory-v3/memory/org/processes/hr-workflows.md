# HR Workflows — Craftech360

*Source: Suman (HR) — 2026-02-06*

---

## A. Attendance Management

- Attendance tracked **daily** (manual tracker / spreadsheet / tool)
- HR reviews attendance **monthly** for reporting
- Notable absences or irregularities escalated to reporting managers

## B. Leave Management

1. Employee informs and gets approval from **Team Lead**
2. HR records approved leaves in tracker
3. WFH requests monitored and approved as per policy

## C. Recruitment Process

| Step | Action | Owner |
|------|--------|-------|
| 1 | Requirement shared by department head | Dept Head |
| 2 | HR creates JD & posts on LinkedIn, WhatsApp groups, Instagram | HR |
| 3 | Applications collected via Google Forms / email | HR |
| 4 | Initial screening | HR |
| 5 | Technical / managerial interview | Dept Head |
| 6 | Final approval | Management |
| 7 | Offer / Internship letter shared | HR |

**Posting Channels:**
- LinkedIn
- WhatsApp groups
- Instagram

**Application Collection:**
- Google Forms
- Email

## D. Onboarding Process

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Offer / Internship letter issued |
| 2 | Signed acceptance collected |
| 3 | Joining formalities (basic details, documents) |
| 4 | Introduction to team & reporting manager |
| 5 | Access to tools, systems, and trackers |
| 6 | Probation / internship tracking |

## E. Payroll & Compensation

- Monthly salary processing coordinated with accounts
- HR communicates delays or updates proactively
- Intern stipends handled separately
- **No mid-cycle revisions** unless approved by management

## F. Employee Engagement

- Snacks & games arranged periodically
- Team outings / POSH training suggested
- Feedback collected through forms when required

## Daily HR Operations

### Morning
- Attendance check
- Responding to employee queries
- Follow-ups on leave/WFH approvals

### Mid-Day
- Recruitment activities (screening, calls, scheduling interviews)
- Coordination with department heads
- Documentation & tracker updates

### Afternoon
- Onboarding/offboarding tasks
- Policy clarifications
- Payroll or admin coordination

### End of Day
- Status updates
- Pending follow-ups list
- Preparation for next day's tasks
