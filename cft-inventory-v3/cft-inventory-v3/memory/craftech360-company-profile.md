### NEBULA (3D Volumetric Display)

- Visible from any side (true 3D)
- Fully customizable
- Lights dance to music
- Available in 4, 9, or 10 module configurations

**Use Cases:** Business gatherings, brand activation, product launches, festivals

### AURORA (Flexible 2D LED)

- Made from linear LED strips
- Can be shaped to custom designs
- Software controlled for dynamic content
- Integrated sensors for interactivity
- 17 feet installation at TN Global Investment Meet

**Use Cases:** Immersive visual experiences, custom-shaped displays

### HOLOFAN (Holographic POV Technology)

- Projects visuals that appear to float in midair
- Gesture-based sensors
- Improved image clarity
- Can display showreels, audiovisuals, 3D elements
- Reacts to gestures

**Use Cases:** Showreels, brand promotion, exhibitions, product launches, museums

### INTERACTIVE PROJECTION MAPPING

- Any flat surface becomes interactive visual feast
- Walls, floors, objects
- Customizable to brand themes
- Storytelling capabilities

**Use Cases:** Product launches, art installations, exhibits, storytelling

### ROTOSCOPE (Transparent Sliding Screen)

- Layered visual storytelling
- Tablet-activated
- Touchless navigation
- Dynamic content layering

**Case Study:** ELECRAMA 2025 - Centerpiece of Raychem RPG's award-winning booth

### AR NAVIGATION

- Real-time augmented reality wayfinding
- No downloads needed - QR code scan
- Interactive AR arrows on phone
- Multilingual support

**Case Study:** G20 Summit - Helped thousands of global delegates navigate

### DIGITAL LIBRARY

- Replaces paper brochures
- Email input → personalized content
- No paper, no clutter
- Powerful follow-up for brands

**Case Study:** Dell Technologies Forum
