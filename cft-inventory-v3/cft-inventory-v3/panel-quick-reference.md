## 📖 YOUR 4 STORIES (30 Seconds Each)

### 1. Aurora — "CAD vs Physics"
> "17-foot curved LED installation. 3.5 lakh pixels. CAD said perfect. Three fabrication attempts later, we learned LEDs don't bend like software predicts. AI could generate the content. But the physics? That required iteration."

### 2. DNA Structure — "Motors Colliding"
> "Three rotating LED layers. Animation software showed perfect choreography. Reality showed motors colliding. We wrote custom algorithms to track momentum, not just position. That algorithm is now in three other installations."

### 3. Rotoscope — "The Humidity Fail"
> "ELECRAMA event. Worked perfectly at 2 AM. At 2 PM with 500 people watching? Humidity killed the sensors. We diagnosed, adapted to backup mode in 10 minutes, and won the award. Not for the installation — for the recovery."

### 4. MetaShot — "40 Iterations"
> "Smart cricket bat. 40+ design iterations over 18 months. Each prototype cost ₹50K-₹1L. No 're-render' button. Shark Tank India, ₹10Cr revenue, 20K units. Hardware iteration is expensive, slow, and humbling."
