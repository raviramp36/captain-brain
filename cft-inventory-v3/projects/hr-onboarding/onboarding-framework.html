Framework Purpose
Comprehensive employee lifecycle management

This framework establishes a robust onboarding process and employee protection system for Craftech360, addressing:

  • Complete document collection & KYC verification
  • Employment agreements & legal protection
  • Confidentiality & information security
  • Company asset protection (including SIM cards)
  • Structured induction & integration program
🚨

Critical Issue Addressed

Recent incidents of competitor poaching and internal leaks of client information, financial data, and salary details. This framework implements strict controls and legally binding agreements.

Legal Review Required

All documents should be reviewed by a labor lawyer for enforceability under Indian law and Karnataka Shops & Establishments Act compliance before final implementation.

HIGH PRIORITY

📜 1. NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement)

Comprehensive protection of all confidential business information

  • Definitions (Confidential Information scope)
  • Client Information protection
  • Financial Information protection
  • Salary & HR Information protection
  • Business & Technical Information
  • Employee Obligations
  • Term: During + 3 years post-employment
  • Remedies: Injunction + Damages
HIGH PRIORITY

🚫 2. Non-Compete & Non-Solicitation

Block competitor poaching and client stealing

  • Non-Compete (during employment)
  • Non-Solicitation of Employees (12-24 mo)
  • Non-Solicitation of Clients (12-24 mo)
  • Moonlighting Prohibition
  • Named Competitors List
  • Indemnification clause
HIGH PRIORITY

📱 3. Company SIM Card Agreement

Protect directors from SIM misuse liability

  • SIM Details & Assignment
  • Acknowledgment (Director's name on SIM)
  • Permitted Use (business only)
  • Prohibited Activities
  • Employee Liability clause
  • Director Indemnification
  • Return & Handover policy
  • Monitoring Notice
HIGH PRIORITY

💰 4. Salary Confidentiality Declaration

Stop internal salary discussions and leaks

  • Declaration of confidentiality
  • Non-Disclosure of own salary
  • Non-Inquiry of others' salary
  • Non-Discussion policy
  • Limited Exceptions
  • Consequences (Warning → Termination)
MEDIUM PRIORITY

🔐 5. Information Security Policy

Rules for handling company data and systems

  • Data Classification (4 levels)
  • Device Security rules
  • Data Handling rules
  • Communication rules
  • Access Control
  • Social Media policy
  • Incident Reporting
  • Monitoring Notice
MEDIUM PRIORITY

📋 6. Code of Conduct

Behavioral and ethical standards

  • Professional Conduct
  • Conflict of Interest
  • Company Resources use
  • Attendance & Punctuality
  • Confidentiality reference
  • Compliance requirements
  • Disciplinary Matrix
MEDIUM PRIORITY

📃 7. Employment Agreement

Master employment contract

  • Position & Duties
  • Compensation & Benefits
  • Working Hours & Leave
  • Probation Period
  • Termination Clauses
  • NDA & IP Assignment reference
  • Non-Compete reference
  • Dispute Resolution
MEDIUM PRIORITY

🚪 8. Exit Undertaking

Secure company interests at separation

  • Employee & Separation Details
  • Return of Property checklist
  • Data Deletion confirmation
  • Pending Obligations cleared
  • Reaffirmation of NDA (3 years)
  • Non-Solicitation reminder (12-24 mo)
  • Forwarding Contact details

Implementation Plan
Phased rollout approach

📌 Phase 1: New Employees

Immediate implementation

  • All documents mandatory from Day 1
  • No exceptions - part of joining process
  • Cannot start work without signatures
  • HR to maintain signed document folder

📌 Phase 2: Existing Employees

Within 2 weeks

  • HR conducts group sessions
  • Explain need: "Protecting everyone"
  • Mandatory signing within deadline
  • Refusals escalated to Founders

📌 Phase 3: Legal Review

Before deployment

  • All documents reviewed by labor lawyer
  • Karnataka compliance check
  • Enforceability validation
  • Final approval from Founders
