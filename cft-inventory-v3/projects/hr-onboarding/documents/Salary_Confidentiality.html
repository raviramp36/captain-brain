DECLARATION

I, _______________________________, hereby acknowledge and declare the following:

  1. Confidentiality of Own Salary:
    I understand and agree that my salary, compensation, benefits, bonuses, incentives, and all other components of my remuneration package are strictly confidential. I shall NOT disclose my salary details to any colleague, former employee, or external party.
  2. Non-Inquiry of Others' Salary:
    I shall NOT seek, inquire about, or attempt to obtain salary information of any other employee of Craftech360, whether current or former. This includes direct questioning, indirect probing, or accessing unauthorized records.
  3. Non-Discussion:
    I shall NOT discuss, compare, or speculate about salary matters with colleagues, whether formally or informally, in person or through any communication medium including messages, emails, or social media.
  4. Rationale:
    I understand that salary is determined based on various factors including role, experience, performance, market conditions, and business requirements. Salary comparisons can lead to misunderstandings, dissatisfaction, and workplace discord. The Company's decision on compensation is final and confidential.
  5. Permitted Discussions:
    I may discuss my salary ONLY with:
    • HR Department for clarifications regarding my own salary;
    • Finance/Accounts for tax-related queries;
    • My immediate family members (spouse/parents), with the advisory to maintain confidentiality;
    • Tax consultants or legal advisors bound by professional confidentiality;
    • Government authorities as required by law.

⚠️ CONSEQUENCES OF BREACH

I understand and acknowledge that violation of this declaration will be treated as a serious breach of Company policy and my employment terms. Consequences may include:

  • First Offense: Written warning and counseling
  • Second Offense: Final warning and possible suspension
  • Third Offense or Serious Breach: Termination of employment

In case of malicious intent or if the breach causes significant harm to the Company or other employees, the Company reserves the right to take immediate termination action and pursue legal remedies.

UNDERTAKING

I hereby undertake and confirm that:

  1. I have read and fully understood this Salary Confidentiality Declaration;
  2. I agree to abide by the confidentiality requirements stated herein;
  3. I understand that this declaration is a condition of my employment;
  4. I will immediately report to HR if I become aware of any breach of salary confidentiality by myself or others;
  5. I accept the consequences outlined above in case of any breach;
  6. This undertaking shall remain in effect during my employment and for a period of 2 (two) years after termination.
