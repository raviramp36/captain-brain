DECLARATION

I, [Employee_Name], Employee ID [Employee_ID], hereby acknowledge and agree to the following:

1. CONFIDENTIALITY OF COMPENSATION

1.1 I understand that my compensation, including but not limited to salary, bonuses, allowances, benefits, stock options, and any other forms of remuneration (collectively "Compensation Information"), is strictly confidential.

1.2 I agree that Compensation Information is the proprietary information of [Company_Name] and is shared with me on a need-to-know basis.

2. NON-DISCLOSURE OBLIGATIONS

2.1 I shall NOT:

  • Discuss my salary, CTC, or any component of my compensation with colleagues, other employees, or any person within the organization (except HR and authorized personnel)
  • Disclose my Compensation Information to any external party, including friends, family, or acquaintances who may communicate such information to Company employees
  • Share, display, or allow access to my salary slips, offer letters, appraisal letters, or any compensation-related documents
  • Inquire about or attempt to obtain information regarding other employees' compensation
  • Post, share, or discuss compensation details on social media, job portals, forums, or any public or private platforms
  • Use compensation information for negotiation or comparison purposes within the organization

3. RATIONALE

3.1 I understand that salary confidentiality is essential because:

  • Compensation is determined based on individual qualifications, experience, performance, and market factors
  • Disclosure can lead to misunderstandings, workplace conflicts, and reduced morale
  • It protects the privacy of all employees
  • It maintains a professional work environment

4. CONSEQUENCES OF BREACH

4.1 I understand that violation of this declaration may result in:

Offense Consequence
First Offense Verbal Warning with documentation
Second Offense Written Warning in employee file
Third Offense Final Warning / Suspension
Repeated/Serious Violation Termination of employment

4.2 The Company reserves the right to take appropriate action based on the severity of the breach, which may include immediate termination in cases of willful or malicious disclosure.

5. EXCEPTIONS

5.1 This declaration does not prevent me from:

  • Discussing my compensation with HR for legitimate queries or concerns
  • Providing compensation information to authorized personnel as required for official purposes
  • Disclosing information as required by law or legal proceedings (with prior notice to the Company where possible)
  • Discussing compensation with my own legal or financial advisors under confidentiality

6. DURATION

6.1 This obligation shall remain in effect throughout my employment and shall survive termination for a period of one (1) year thereafter.

7. ACKNOWLEDGMENT

I confirm that:

  • I have read and understood this Salary Confidentiality Declaration
  • I agree to abide by all terms stated herein
  • I understand the consequences of violating this declaration
  • This declaration is part of my terms of employment
