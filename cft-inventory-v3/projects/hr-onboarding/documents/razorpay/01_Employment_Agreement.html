2.1 This employment shall commence on [Employee_Joining_Date].

  • Either party may terminate the employment with [Probation_Notice] written notice or payment in lieu thereof
  • The Company shall evaluate the Employee's performance, conduct, and suitability

  • Statutory contributions (PF, ESI as applicable)
  • Other allowances as per Company policy

3.3 Salary shall be paid monthly, by the [Salary_Pay_Date] of each month, after deduction of applicable taxes and statutory contributions.

3.4 The Employee shall be entitled to benefits as per Company policy, which may be modified from time to time at the Company's discretion.

4.1 The Employee shall work [Working_Hours] hours per week, typically Monday through Saturday, as per the schedule determined by the Company.

4.3 The Employee shall maintain regular attendance and punctuality. Unauthorized absence may result in disciplinary action and salary deduction.

5.1 The Employee shall be entitled to [Leave_Days] days of paid leave per annum, in addition to public holidays as per the Company's holiday calendar.

  • Business requirements and team availability

5.3 Unused leave may be carried forward or encashed as per Company policy.

6. DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES

6.1 The Employee shall:

  • Perform duties assigned diligently and to the best of their ability
  • Follow lawful instructions of supervisors and management
  • Devote full working time and attention to Company business
  • Act in the best interests of the Company at all times
  • Maintain professional conduct and uphold Company reputation
  • Comply with all Company policies, procedures, and codes

6.2 The Company reserves the right to modify the Employee's duties, designation, department, or reporting structure based on business needs, with reasonable notice.

7. EXCLUSIVITY OF SERVICE

7.1 During employment, the Employee shall not:

  • Engage in any other employment, business, or profession without prior written approval
  • Accept any position (paid or unpaid) with any other organization
  • Provide consulting or advisory services to any third party
  • Engage in any activity that conflicts with Company interests

7.2 The Employee shall disclose any existing business interests or external commitments prior to joining.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY

8.1 The Employee acknowledges that during employment, they will have access to Confidential Information including but not limited to:

  • Client information, contracts, and project details
  • Financial data, pricing, and business strategies
  • Technical designs, source code, and methodologies
  • Employee data and compensation information
  • Trade secrets and proprietary processes

8.2 The Employee agrees to:

  • Maintain strict confidentiality of all such information
  • Not disclose to any third party without authorization
  • Use such information solely for Company purposes
  • Return all confidential materials upon termination

8.3 These confidentiality obligations survive termination of employment for a period of three (3) years.

9. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

9.1 All work product, inventions, designs, code, creative works, and intellectual property created by the Employee during employment or using Company resources shall be the exclusive property of the Company.

9.2 The Employee hereby assigns all rights, title, and interest in such intellectual property to the Company and waives all moral rights therein.

9.3 The Employee shall execute any documents necessary to perfect the Company's ownership of such intellectual property.

10. NON-COMPETE

10.1 During employment and for a period of [Non_Compete_Period] after termination, the Employee shall not:

  • Join or work with any competing business
  • Start or be involved in any competing venture
  • Provide services to competitors directly or indirectly

10.2 Competitors include companies in the experiential technology, kinetic installations, interactive displays, AI experiences, and event technology industry, including but not limited to: Jaseil Creations, 4Brains, Tagbin, MagicTap, InkinnCaps, Attah, and Digital Jalebi.

11. NON-SOLICITATION

11.1 For a period of twelve (12) months following termination, the Employee shall not:

  • Solicit, recruit, or induce any Company employee to leave employment
  • Hire or engage any person who was a Company employee in the preceding 12 months
  • Solicit or divert any client or customer with whom the Employee had dealings
  • Interfere with any business relationship of the Company

12. TERMINATION

12.1 During Probation: Either party may terminate with [Probation_Notice] notice or payment in lieu.

12.2 After Confirmation: Either party may terminate by providing [Notice_Period] written notice or payment of gross salary in lieu thereof.

12.3 Termination for Cause: The Company may terminate immediately without notice for:

  • Gross misconduct or dishonesty
  • Breach of confidentiality or Company policies
  • Conviction of a criminal offense
  • Willful negligence causing harm to Company
  • Repeated poor performance despite warnings

12.4 Upon termination, the Employee shall:

  • Complete proper handover of duties and projects
  • Return all Company property, documents, and assets
  • Delete Company data from personal devices
  • Cooperate with exit procedures
